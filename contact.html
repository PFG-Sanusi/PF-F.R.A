Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions Answered

Find answers to the most common questions about our services and platform.

How long does a typical flood risk assessment take?

Most assessments are completed within 2-4 weeks, depending on the complexity and size of the area. We provide detailed timelines during our initial consultation and can expedite urgent requests when needed.

What data do you need to conduct an assessment?

We primarily use satellite imagery, weather data, and topographic information. For more detailed analysis, we may request additional local data or conduct site visits. Our team will guide you through the data collection process.

Do you provide services internationally?

Yes, we provide flood risk assessment services globally. Our satellite imagery and modeling capabilities work worldwide, and we have experience working with clients across different continents and regulatory environments.

What formats do you deliver results in?

We provide results in multiple formats including PDF reports, GIS shapefiles, KML files, and interactive web dashboards. We can also customize the delivery format to match your specific requirements and existing systems.

Is there a free consultation available?

Yes, we offer free initial consultations to discuss your needs and provide preliminary recommendations. This helps us understand your requirements and provide accurate project estimates.

Do you offer ongoing monitoring services?

Yes, we provide continuous monitoring services with regular updates and alerts for changing flood risk conditions. Our monitoring solutions include real-time alerts, monthly reports, and annual assessments.
