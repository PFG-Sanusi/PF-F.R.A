How to Use the AOI Tool

1

Select Drawing Tool

Click "Draw Area" to activate polygon drawing mode

2

Draw Your Area

Click on the map to create polygon points. Double-click to finish.

Minimum area: 1 km² (100 hectares or 1,000,000 m²)
3

Submit Request

Click "Submit Request" to send your AOI for professional analysis. You'll receive a confirmation email with your request ID.

4

Receive Results

Our experts will analyze your area and email you detailed results within 2-5 business days. No instant processing - all analyses are done by professionals.
