About Proforce Galaxies

Pioneering comprehensive space operations across the entire value chain, from spacecraft manufacturing to integrated surveillance solutions, empowering Nigeria's space capabilities with indigenous innovation.

Our Company

Indigenous Space Technology Solutions for Complete Value Chain Operations

Proforce Galaxies stands as Nigeria's premier indigenous space technology provider, delivering comprehensive solutions across upstream, midstream, and downstream space operations. As part of Proforce Limited, we leverage our parent company's pioneering legacy in defense and security innovation to build complete space capabilities.

Our integrated approach encompasses everything from spacecraft manufacturing and ground station equipment to satellite data services and mobile communications solutions. Through indigenous satellite systems and strategic partnerships, we deliver end-to-end space operations that ensure Nigeria's sovereignty in critical space infrastructure.
